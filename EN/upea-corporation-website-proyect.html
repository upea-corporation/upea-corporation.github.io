UPEA Corporation is a Community Wikipedia Project inspired by the SCP universe, but with a focus on anthropomorphic entities. The website presents itself as a secret government corporation whose mission is the protection and coexistence of humanity and Zoomorphic Anthropoid Entities (ZAE).

What is the UPEA universe about?

This project is set in a fictional universe where, after World War II, a "Hidden War" occurred between humans and intelligent entities with animal traits. Decades later, UPEA Corporation was born, an organization that, unlike others, does not seek containment, but rather the study and protection of these entities. Its motto is "We Do Not Hunt, We Study and Protect". [MORE INFORMATION]

We cannot provide much information regarding the Hidden War, as such information is classified by UPEA Corporation. Research is required to find the method of access to said information, by the user.

How can you participate?

The website functions as a platform for users to participate in this universe. You can register in two ways:

  • As a Zoomorphic Anthropoid Entity (ZAE): If you register as a ZAE, you can create the story and lore of your fursona, detailing its origins and abilities. You have the freedom to modify, add or delete your information at any time.
  • As a Corporation Employee: If you register as an employee, your information will be encoded exclusively in case you cease to be part of this UPEA Corporation universe. The employee's information cannot be suppressed or deleted.

The site includes hidden accesses that require codes, which are found on the social networks and the UPEA Corporation website itself, making the experience more interactive for users who enjoy research. All registration terms and conditions are located in the footer.

Mission and Ethics

UPEA Corporation is dedicated to the identification, study and protection of anomalous phenomena and entities, especially those of anthropoid and zoomorphic nature. Its objective is to ensure peaceful coexistence between humans and these entities, treating ZAE not as objects of containment, but as conscious beings with complex sensitivities. This commitment to ethics and coexistence is a fundamental pillar of the corporation and its narrative universe.
