UPEA Corporation: A Universe of Fiction üêæ

UPEA Corporation is an alternate reality game (ARG) project and a community wiki inspired by the SCP universe, but with a focus on anthropomorphic entities. The website presents itself as a secret government corporation whose mission is the protection and coexistence of humanity and Zoomorphic Anthropoid Entities (ZAE).

What is the UPEA universe about?

This project is set in a fictional universe where, after World War II, a "Hidden War" occurred between humans and intelligent entities with animal traits. Decades later, UPEA Corporation was born, an organization that, unlike others, does not seek containment, but rather the study and protection of these entities. Its motto is "We Don't Hunt, We Study and Protect". [MORE INFORMATION]

We cannot provide much information about the Hidden War, as this information is classified by UPEA Corporation. Research is required by the user to find the method of accessing this information.

How can you participate?

The website functions as a platform for users to participate in this universe. You can register in two ways:

  • As a Zoomorphic Anthropoid Entity (ZAE): If you register as a ZAE, you can create the story and "lore" of your fursona, detailing their origins and abilities. You have the freedom to modify, add, or delete your information at any time.
  • As a Corporation Employee: If you register as an employee, your information will be exclusively encoded in case you cease to be part of this UPEA Corporation universe. The employee's information cannot be suppressed or deleted.

The site includes hidden access points that require codes, which can be found on social media and on UPEA Corporation's website itself, making the experience more interactive for users who enjoy investigation. All registration terms and conditions can be found in the footer.

Mission and Ethics

UPEA Corporation is dedicated to the identification, study, and protection of anomalous phenomena and entities, especially those of anthropoid and zoomorphic nature. Its goal is to ensure peaceful coexistence between humans and these entities, treating ZAE not as objects of containment, but as conscious beings with complex sensibilities. This commitment to ethics and coexistence is a fundamental pillar of the corporation and its narrative universe.
