Discarded Content of UPEA Corp.

This file serves as a storage for the content that has been removed from the main website, but which is desired to be preserved for future reference or possible reuse.

First Design of the Website

Date: 16 of May of 2025 (16:50 UTC+2)

The initial design of the website marked the starting point for UPEA Corp. in its digital development. Although this first prototype was simple and rudimentary, it set the foundation for a significant evolution. From this first iteration, a more attractive design was achieved, and the organization was expanded to accommodate the creation of additional pages.

Slogan of UPEA Corp.

Date: 16 of May of 2025 (16:52 - 17:31 UTC+2)

In the search for a slogan that would adequately represent UPEA Corporation, various sources were explored, including existing references and popular concepts in the realm of fandoms. For a period, the slogan "Anthropomorphic Animals Are... Enhanced Humans?" was considered. However, after further evaluation, it was decided to adopt "We Don't Hunt, We Study and Contain" as the official slogan of UPEA Corp., considering it more appropriate and representative of the organisation's identity.

Notes: On May 20, 2025, at 19:41 UTC, a change was made to the slogan of UPEA Corporation. The phrase "We Don't Hunt, We Study and Protect" was officially adopted as a permanent change, as it was considered the option that best defined and represented the mission and values of our organization.

Clear and Published Design

Date: 18-20 of May of 2025

After a series of design adjustments, the initial development of the UPEA Corp. website began to take shape. At this stage, the site was hosted free of charge on the Netlify service. However, at that time, access to this development version was limited to a small number of people. It should be noted that the main information about UPEA Corp. was located on the home page, unlike its current location in a dedicated section.

Notes: On May 18, 2025, at 14:43 UTC+2, an attempt was made to relocate the navigation menu section to a vertical layout. However, after evaluation, it was determined that the horizontal and centered position at the top of the page was the most appropriate option.

Containment Measures

Date: 29 of May of 2025 (19:20 UTC+2)

Previously, containment measures had been implemented for each ZAE. However, due to the absence of a clear organization in the containment protocols and an unsuccessful attempt to establish them, these measures had to be removed. This decision also aligns with the adoption of the phrase "Protect", which reflects an approach that does not include containment.

Image: The entity ZAE#666 had implemented containment measures.

Codex of UPEA Corp.

Date: 22 of May of 2025 (13:05 UTC+2)

UPEA Corp. has implemented a proprietary text encoding system, designed based on existing encoding principles. We currently have three different encodings for specific information. Initially, there was only one system with operational flaws and a Matrix-themed design that inserted a false character into the encoding. Although the decoding was correct, these shortcomings forced us to completely redesign the system, while retaining the original Matrix aesthetic.

Notes: Prior to the implementation of the "ALPHA" encoding system, which had security limitations (even with the insertion of a false character), the "UPEA Codex" subdepartment was established. This initiative aimed to expand and improve our encoding capabilities.

Icon of UPEA Corp.

Date: 16 of May of 2025 (12:22 UTC+2)

The original logo or icon of UPEA Corp. was conceived based on the figure of a jaguar and robotics elements. This was the first design used during the initial stages of the website development. It is worth noting that, in the lower part, the logo incorporated the name "UPEA" with a custom metal typography.

6 of May of 2025 (12:17 UTC+2)

Notes: Originally, the design in question was intended to serve as a cover for an interactive book with wiki functions about EAU (Entidades Antropomórficas Únicas) and in English it would be UAE "Unique Anthropomorphic Entities", and not as a logo or icon of an organization. At that time, a few days later, a more professional web development started, mainly a closed website for close friends.

Informative Audios

Date: 13 of July of 2025 (16:19 UTC+2)

UPEA Corp. considered the implementation of informative audios on its website. However, this idea was later discarded. It was predicted that informative videos would be published on UPEA Corp.'s official YouTube channel.

Notes: On June 2, 2025, after extensive research on the format and considering the limited experience in producing them, the idea was discarded. Currently, its implementation is considered as a possibility for a long-term future, or even null.

New Logo and Icon of UPEA Corp.

Date: 04 of August of 2025 (15:40 UTC+2)

After evaluating two versions of the logo and icon, one with a background and another without, the second version was selected. This decision was made to enhance the professionalism of the brand and ensure a better visual integration in various supports. The logo and icon version with a background has been discontinued in favor of the version without a background, which optimizes convenience and professional integration of the design.

Notes: Initially, it was considered to use the version with a background for the browser tab and the version without a background for the header.

Postponed Updates Section

Date: 2 September 2025 (15:08 UTC+2)

In order to optimise content organisation, the “Postponed Updates” section has been removed. As new features and content are expected to be added in the future, we have determined that the "Future Updates" section is the most appropriate place to manage and present this information optimally on the UPEA Corporation website, even though the release dates are undefined.

Notes: This image corresponds to 2 September 2025, at 15:08 UTC+2, before the removal of the "Postponed Updates" section. The screenshot reflects the most recent update to the UPEA Corporation website on that date.

Complete Redesign of The Website

Date: 06 of September 2025 (16:35 UTC+2)

After an evaluation, we have decided not to carry out a complete redesign of the UPEA Corporation website. At this moment, we do not consider it viable due to the volume of content. We believe that the current design is more than adequate and functional, both visually and in the context of the corporation.
