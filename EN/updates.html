Latest Updates of UPEA Corp.

NOTICE! All updates are recorded locally before publication since May 26, 2025. To optimize files, we will summarize monthly updates after each month. And starting from September 21, 2025, updates will be recorded with updates dedicated to translated pages in their corresponding language instead of being done generally. We will also save a lot of text to read, only on the Spanish page you will be able to see all the monthly information of the year without summarizing.

2025

►

Summary Updates for May 2025

  • Spelling and file name corrections were made.
  • Syntax issues were fixed and the administrative database was modified.
  • The "Containment Measures" section was removed from entity information and the administrative access method was readjusted.
  • Information was added to the first entity registered in the corporation.
  • The updates page was implemented and visual improvements were made to the top bar and image loading.

Summary Updates for June 2025

  • The database was updated and the first personnel file was incorporated.
  • Twitter/X social network redirection was fixed and a custom redirection for error 404 was implemented.
  • An access issue from ZAE entity files was fixed and the interview for entity ZAE#000 was added.
  • Security, privacy and cookies policy information was updated.
  • The use of auth0 extension was removed.
  • The "Register Entities" section was implemented.

Summary Updates for July 2025

  • Mobile device design was optimized and fixed in various sections, including the dropdown menu and entity files.
  • Copyright and license policies were updated.
  • The file for entity ZAE#000-1 was implemented and the file design was updated.
  • The "Discarded Content" about web development origins was recovered and updated.
  • Entity ZAE#666-1 was registered and UPEA Corporation general information was modified.
  • The Octokit REST library update to version 22.0.0 was completed.
  • The new entity ZAE#201 was added and its information was updated, also removing the direct message bubble in the files.

Summary Updates for August 2025

  • New reports were added and errors were fixed in July reports and in the interview of entity ZAE#201.
  • Information for entities ZAE#201 and ZAE#666-1 was updated.
  • Language translations and information about UPEA Corporation origins were re-implemented.
  • The Updates page display was optimized and fixed for mobile devices.
  • Files have been migrated to UPEA Corporation organization's GitHub repository.
  • The "Reports" page was implemented and credential data integrity issues were fixed.
  • UPEA Corporation logo and icon were updated and erroneous links were fixed.

Summary Updates for September 2025

  • A design issue affecting the display of unique species definitions on mobile devices was fixed.
  • Classification was unified so that preliminary classifications are now considered normal classification.
  • The new entity ZAE#001 was registered in the UPEA Corporation database.
  • Entity ZAE#666-1 was updated with a unique species name.
  • A new system was implemented that allows ZAE entities possessing unique species names to view the definition of their respective species.
  • Lastly, a change was made to infrastructure nomenclature: the name of Sector Number 4 and one of its departments was changed, removing any mention of Containment.
