We Do Not Hunt, We Study and Protect

At UPEA Corp., our commitment goes beyond protection. We believe in a future where harmonious coexistence between humans and anthropomorphic beings is the norm. Our mission is to build a safe and comprehensible home for those with an exceptional singularity.

Explore how we work to create a respectful, studying, and mutual growth environment. Because every being, regardless of its origin, deserves a place where to thrive.

Secure Integration

We facilitate the adaptation and well-being of anthropomorphic beings in controlled environments, ensuring their security and privacy.

Study and Understanding

Promote ethical research to understand their origins, abilities, and culture, fostering mutual knowledge.

Well-being and Development

We offer resources for personal development and artistic expression, ensuring a fulfilling and enriching life.

Legal Protection

We work to establish legal frameworks that safeguard their rights and prevent discrimination or exploitation.

Community and Support

We create spaces for interaction and support between anthropomorphic beings and allied humans, fostering trust and connection.

Confidentiality

We operate with the utmost discretion to protect the identity and existence of anthropomorphic entities.
