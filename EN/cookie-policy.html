UPEA Corporation Cookie Policy

At UPEA Corporation we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we use cookies on our website and how you can manage them.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember information about your visit and ensure the proper functioning of the page.

Cookies We Use

Our site only uses strictly necessary cookies, that is, those essential for the operation of the website and the services it provides. In particular:

  • Cookies for the operation of the website hosted on Netlify.
  • Cookies that enable the correct processing and submission of Google Forms (for entity, employee, or feedback registrations).

We do not use tracking, advertising, third-party analytics, or marketing cookies.

Legal Basis

The use of strictly necessary cookies is carried out under the legal basis of legitimate interest, as they are essential to enable the operation of our site and the forms used by users.

Cookie Management

You can configure your browser to block or delete cookies. Please note that if you disable these essential cookies, some site functions, such as form submission, may not work properly.

Consent

By accessing and browsing our website, you accept the use of strictly necessary cookies for the operation of the page.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this cookie policy at any time. Any relevant changes will be notified on our website and reflected in the "Last updated" date.

Users are responsible for periodically reviewing this Cookie Policy to stay informed of any changes or modifications.
