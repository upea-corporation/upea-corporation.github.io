INTERVIEWER'S CONCLUSION

The entity ZAE#666-2 consists of a set of algorithmic clones derived directly from the primary entity HecTéx. Their consciousness is an extension of that entity's will, acting as a network of interconnected echoes with no real autonomy. Although their power is inferior to the original, their pyro-energetic capabilities remain highly destructive, able to coordinate group attacks and execute instantaneous tele-transfers.

The response pattern reveals complete subordination to the being HecTéx, as well as an absence of self-preservation instinct or independent initiative. Their refusal to answer about weaknesses or limits suggests either programmed ignorance or deliberate censorship. The ability to vanish without leaving physical traces indicates the practical impossibility of containment through conventional methods.

Because their behavior depends on the direct influence of HecTéx and their risk arises mainly from mismanagement of their control or collective manifestation. It requires specialized protocols of observation and indirect isolation, focused on supervision of the matrix entity (HecTéx) rather than on the clones themselves.
