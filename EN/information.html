Who Are We?

We are a secret government organization, established with a single purpose: The safeguarding of coexistence. We operate in the shadows, on the periphery of public perception, to protect humanity from the extraordinary, and the extraordinary from itself.

Our Mission

UPEA Corporation is dedicated to the identification, study, and protection of anomalous phenomena, entities, and artifacts, with a particular focus on those of anthropoid nature and zoomorphic characteristics.

Our motto encapsulates everything: "We Don't Hunt, We Study and Protect"

  • We Don't Hunt: We do not seek eradication or exploitation. Our ethics prohibit unnecessary violence and the instrumentalization of anomalies.
  • We Study: Understanding is our most powerful weapon. Through rigorous research, we seek to unravel the mysteries of the anomalous to ensure everyone's safety.
  • We Protect: We safeguard both society from potential threats that anomalies may represent, and the anomalous entities themselves from ignorance, fear, and possible exploitation by third parties.

Coexistence and Study

Unlike other organizations, UPEA has discovered and classified a unique category of anomalous phenomena: Zoomorphic Anthropoid Entities (ZAE). These entities possess humanoid characteristics combined with animal traits, often exhibiting advanced intelligence, unique abilities, and in some cases, complex emotional and cultural sensitivities.

For UPEA, these ZAE are not mere disposable objects, but beings whose existence challenges our understanding of biology and consciousness. Our approach is nuanced, seeking peaceful coexistence and mutual understanding whenever possible, without compromising global security.

Classification

To manage the diversity of anomalous phenomena, UPEA uses an internal classification system based on potential risk:

  • Umbralis Class: Entities or phenomena that pose a low risk to global security and are relatively easy to adapt.
  • Liminalis Class: Entities or phenomena that require specific protocols and pose a moderate risk if their capabilities are mismanaged.
  • Obscuris Class: Highly dangerous entities or phenomena, or those with unknown capabilities, representing a significant threat to human existence or the stability of reality.
  • Abyssum Class: Entities or phenomena with self-replicating, contagious, or large-scale reality-altering properties.
  • Feralis Class: Entities or phenomena lacking self-awareness and in a wild (almost irrational) state; they must be eradicated at all costs, or attempts must be made to control them.

Protocols and Procedures

UPEA operates under a strict set of protocols to ensure safety and discretion:

  • Silent Collection: Location and securing of anomalies with minimal public disturbance.
  • Comfort: UPEA Corporation offers extensive protected properties, adapted to the specific needs of each ZAE, ensuring an environment free from third-party intervention.
  • Ethical Research: Deep scientific and psychological studies to understand ZAE, their origins, and their capabilities.
  • Information Classification: Data access in a strict security hierarchy system.
  • Amnestics and Camouflage: Selective use of amnestic agents and camouflage operations to keep the existence of the anomalous secret.

Our Commitment

In a world where the extraordinary manifests in increasingly complex ways, UPEA Corp remains firm in its commitment: to protect humanity without sacrificing understanding or respect for what we don't understand. We operate at the boundaries of science and morality, for the good of all.

Our work extends beyond mere containment; we seek coexistence, deep understanding of the anomalous, and safeguarding the delicate balance between the known and unknown. Each anomalous entity, especially Zoomorphic Anthropoid Entities (ZAE), is treated with the scientific rigor and ethical consideration that their uniqueness demands. We strive to unravel their mysteries and, when possible, facilitate harmonious integration into reality, away from fear and exploitation. Our oath is to discretion, protection, and truth, ensuring that the existence of the anomalous does not disturb world peace, while cultivating a future where the diversity of life, in all its forms, can thrive safely.
