We Don't Hunt, We Study and Protect

At UPEA Corp., our commitment goes beyond protection. We believe in a future where harmonious coexistence between humans and anthropomorphic beings is the norm. Our mission is to build a safe and compassionate home for those with exceptional singularity.

Discover how we work to foster an environment of respect, study, and mutual growth. Because every being, regardless of their origin, deserves a place to thrive.

Secure Integration

We facilitate the adaptation and well-being of anthropomorphic beings in controlled environments, ensuring their safety and privacy.

Study and Understanding

We promote ethical research to understand their origins, abilities, and culture, fostering mutual knowledge.

Well-being and Development

We offer resources for personal development and artistic expression, ensuring a full and enriching life.

Legal Protection

We work to establish legal frameworks that safeguard their rights and prevent discrimination or exploitation.

Community and Support

We create spaces for interaction and support between anthropomorphic individuals and human allies, fostering bonds of trust.

Confidentiality

We operate with the utmost discretion to protect the identity and existence of anthropomorphic entities.
