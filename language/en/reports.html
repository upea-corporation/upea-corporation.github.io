Annual Report of Activity ZAE

Date: 22 of August of 2025 (19:25 UTC+2)

Type of Report: Attacks on the Installation Cala

Summary: The UPEA Corporation facility at Cala was attacked by entity ZAE#000-1. This event was triggered by an unauthorised experiment conducted by a research team, which placed surveillance on the home of entity ZAE#000, provoking its fury. There was no serious damage to the facility, but the entire surveillance system was destroyed. During the attack, the entity's anomalous abilities were briefly studied, suggesting the need for further research for possible classification as class Zeta. Currently, an internal investigation has been opened to determine those responsible for the experiment and to reassess security protocols.

Annual Report of Possible Vulnerability

Date: 5 of August of 2025 (08:13 UTC+2)

Type of Report: Technical Incident

Summary: During the working day, when starting the system to continue working on the UPEA Corporation website, a visual anomaly was detected in the project's image directory. It has been observed that the thumbnails in the folder are not rendering correctly, displaying a black background in the folder icon box. Although the possibility of unauthorised intervention or access by an ZAE entity has been considered, no conclusive evidence has been found to support this hypothesis. The most likely cause is believed to be an operating system malfunction or security breach. Therefore, the implementation of additional precautions to protect our systems from potential vulnerabilities exploited by EAZs is recommended.

Annual Report of Migration

Date: 5 of August of 2025 (13:08-21:05 UTC+2)

Type of Report: Migration of Files

Summary: The migration of files to the UPEA Corporation GitHub organization repository has been successfully completed.
