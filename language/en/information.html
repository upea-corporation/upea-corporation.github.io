Who We Are?

We are a secret government organization, established with a single purpose: the safeguarding of coexistence. We operate in the shadows, on the periphery of public perception, to protect humanity from the extraordinary, and the extraordinary from itself.

Note: The name UPEA is the Spanish acronym for "Unidad de Protección Especial Antropoide" (Special Anthropoid Protection Unit). We have translated it into English for the user's convenience.

Our Mission

UPEA Corporation is dedicated to the identification, study, and protection of phenomena, entities, and artifacts of an anomalous nature, with a particular focus on those of a Zoomorphic Anthropoid Entities.

Our motto encapsulates everything: "We Don't Hunt, We Study and Protect"

  • We Don't Hunt: We do not seek eradication or exploitation. Our ethics prohibit unnecessary violence and the instrumentalization of anomalies.
  • We Study: Understanding is our most potent weapon. Through rigorous research, we seek to unravel the mysteries of the anomalous to ensure everyone's safety.
  • We Protect: We safeguard society from the potential threats that anomalies may pose, as well as the anomalous entities themselves from ignorance, fear, and potential exploitation by third parties.

Coexistence and Study

Unlike other organizations, UPEA has discovered and classified a unique category of anomalous phenomena: Zoomorphic Anthropoid Entities (ZAE). These entities possess humanoid characteristics combined with animal traits, often exhibiting advanced intelligence, unique abilities, and, in some cases, complex emotional and cultural sensitivities.

For UPEA, these ZAE are not mere disposable objects, but beings whose existence challenges our understanding of biology and consciousness. Our approach is nuanced, seeking peaceful coexistence and mutual understanding whenever possible, without compromising global security.

Classification

To manage the diversity of anomalous phenomena, UPEA uses an internal classification system based on potential risk:

  • Gamma Class (Γ): Entities or phenomena that present a low risk to global security and are relatively easy to adapt.
  • Delta Class (Δ): Entities or phenomena that require specific protocols and present a moderate risk if their capabilities are mismanaged.
  • Epsilon Class (Ε): Highly dangerous entities or phenomena with unknown capabilities that pose a significant threat to human existence or the stability of reality.
  • Zeta Class (Ζ): Entities or phenomena with self-replicating, contagious, or large-scale reality-altering properties.
  • Alpha Class (A): Entities or phenomena that do not possess self-awareness and, in a wild (almost irrational) state, must be eradicated at all costs, or attempts must be made to control them.

Protocols and Procedures

UPEA operates under a strict set of protocols to ensure safety and discretion:

  • Silent Collection: Locating and securing anomalies with minimal public disturbance.
  • Comfort: UPEA Corporation offers extensive protected properties, adapted to the specific needs of each ZAE, ensuring an environment free from third-party intervention.
  • Ethical Research: In-depth scientific and psychological studies to understand the ZAE, their origins, and their capabilities.
  • Information Classification: Data access in a strict security hierarchy system.
  • Amnestics and Camouflage: Selective use of amnestics and camouflage operations to keep the existence of the anomalous a secret.

The Hierarchical and Operational Structure of UPEA Corporation

We operate through a global network of covert facilities and highly specialized personnel, organized into interconnected Directorates and Divisions, each with specific protocols and access levels. Zoomorphic Anthropoid Entities (ZAE) are the primary focus of all their operations.

Our Commitment

In a world where the extraordinary manifests in increasingly complex forms, UPEA Corp. remains firm in its commitment: to protect humanity without sacrificing understanding or respect for that which we do not understand. We operate at the limits of science and morality, for the good of all.

Our work extends beyond mere containment; we seek coexistence, a deep understanding of the anomalous, and the safeguarding of the delicate balance between the known and the unknown. Each anomalous entity, especially the Zoomorphic Anthropoid Entities (ZAE), is treated with the scientific rigor and ethical consideration their uniqueness demands. We strive to unravel their mysteries and, when possible, facilitate a harmonious integration into reality, far from fear and exploitation. Our oath is to discretion, protection, and truth, ensuring that the existence of the anomalous does not disturb world peace, while we cultivate a future where the diversity of life, in all its forms, can thrive safely.
